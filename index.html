the manifesto

Arthouse exists at the threshold between algorithm and intuition, between the pixel and the paradox — a creator base where digital art finds its language.

We believe every pattern holds a philosophy. Every pixel a position. The gallery is not a destination — it is a living document, authored collectively by those who dare to make something beautiful from the noise.
